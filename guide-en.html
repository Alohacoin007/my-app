02

Deposit

Two tabs — Card (buy USDT with a card) / Crypto (you already have USDT). You bet in USDT.

1 · Amount
Type the AMOUNT, or tap $50 · $100 · $500 · $1,000.
2 · Buy USDT
Tap Buy with card · MoonPay (Visa · Mastercard · Apple Pay, instant). If it's not available in your region, use MetaMask.
3 · Send to us
Send the USDT you bought (MoonPay/MetaMask) to the company address — done! The address is in the app (“THEN SEND TO US”); tap Copy.
4 · Submit
Tap Submit deposit request.
5 · Credited
First time only: ID check. Credited once the back office confirms receipt.
Already have USDTUse the Crypto tab — send to the same company address and Submit. Skip the card step.
ImportantSend USDT on the ERC-20 (Ethereum) network — other networks may be lost. After depositing, Transfer it to your Sports account to bet.

04

Your first bet

Tap an odds number on a game, then just enter your stake.

1 · Pick odds
Each game shows 3 markets — Spread (handicap) · Total (Over/Under) · Moneyline (who wins). Tap a number to select it (turns blue).
e.g. Blue Jays ML −124
2 · Open slip
Tap the Bet Slip bar at the bottom.
3 · Mode
Singles (each separate) · Parlay (all must win, bigger odds) · Same Game (combo from one game).
4 · Wager
Enter the Wager. Use +$10 · +$25 · +$100 to add fast, Clear to reset.
5 · Bet!
Check Est. Payout → tap the blue button $20 → win $16.13. Done!
Est. Payout$36.13 = your $20 stake + $16.13 profit. The button shows “stake → profit if it wins.” You get paid only if it wins.

How to read the odds

Odds tell you “how much you win.” (American format)

-140
Minus = favorite (likely to win)
Wins less. Bet $140 → +$100.
+120
Plus = underdog
Wins more. Bet $100 → +$120.
🔒 LockA lock means odds aren't out yet for that game. It opens automatically closer to kickoff — we never make up a fake line.

08

FAQ

Tap a question to expand it.

QWhat is USDT?+
A digital dollar pinned to ~$1 (a stablecoin). It doesn't swing like Bitcoin, so it's safe to move. All Alpexa deposits, bets, and withdrawals use USDT.
QWhat is a crypto wallet?+
An app/account that holds digital money (like a bank account). Each has an 0x… address you use to receive and send. Examples: MetaMask, an exchange wallet (Coinbase, Binance). You enter this address to withdraw. (Alpexa uses the ERC-20 network.)
QWhat is a crypto address?+
The address for receiving and sending crypto — like a bank account number. It starts with 0x… (a long string of letters+numbers, or a QR code). To deposit, send to the company address; to withdraw, enter your own wallet address. ⚠️ One wrong character can't be undone — check it twice!
QWhat is the ERC-20 network?+
The “road” (network) USDT travels on. USDT runs on several (ERC-20, TRC-20…); Alpexa uses ERC-20 (Ethereum). ⚠️ Always send on the same network — other networks may be lost. Choose ERC-20 on your exchange/wallet before sending.
QDoes Alpexa give me a wallet too?+
Yes! Your Alpexa account is itself a USDT wallet. You have a balance on FX · Crypto · Sports, and Transfer moves USDT between them free & instantly. Keep your USDT in Alpexa and transfer to Sports when you want to bet. To take it out, withdraw.
QWhy switch servers (FX/Crypto/Sports)?+
Alpexa has 3 apps (servers) — FX (forex, stocks, indices, metals) · Crypto (coin trading, swap, staking) · Sports (sports betting). Each has a separate balance, so switch to the one you're using. Move money with Transfer (free & instant); bet on the Sports server.
QCan I earn interest on unused USDT?+
Yes! Transfer idle USDT to the Crypto app and stake it to earn interest (APY). USDT: Flexible 3% (unstake anytime) · 90 days 6% · 1 year 9% — longer lock, higher rate. Transfer back to Sports to bet.
QCan I become an Alpexa agent (partner)?+
Yes! The IB (partner) program lets you refer people and earn commission.
① Register a partner account → get your referral link + dashboard
② Share your link → anyone who signs up and trades is tracked to you automatically
③ Commission accrues & pays out automatically — see clicks, sign-ups, volume & commission live (no invoicing).
Multi-asset (FX, stocks, crypto), per-lot commission or revenue share. Start at the IB Portal.
QI deposited but I don't see the balance.+
Deposits appear after the back office confirms receipt (first time: ID check). Also check you used Transfer to move it to your Sports account — it must be there to bet.
QI can't place a bet.+
① Your money must be in the Sports account (use Transfer). ② A 🔒 locked game has no odds yet, so you can't bet it.
QWhat is a Parlay?+
A bet that combines several games. Odds multiply for a much bigger win, but all must win — one miss and it's gone. (Combining picks from one game is a Same Game (SGP).)
QWhat is Same Game (SGP)?+
A parlay within one game (e.g. winner + total + spread). Same game, so all legs must win. (Different games = a regular Parlay.)
QWhat is the Spread?+
A bet with a handicap (points) on the favorite. E.g. −1.5 = that team must win by 2+; +1.5 = that team can lose by 1 or win. It's about how much they win, not just win/lose. (Plain win/lose = Moneyline; combined score = Total.)
QWhat is the Total?+
A bet on whether the two teams' combined score is Over/Under a number. E.g. Over 8.5 = 9+ total, Under 8.5 = 8 or fewer. It doesn't matter who wins — only the score.
QWhat is the Moneyline?+
The simplest bet — just who wins. No handicap or score margin. The favorite shows like −140 (wins less), the underdog +120 (wins more).
QWhy is there a 🔒 lock?+
Odds aren't out yet for that game. It opens automatically closer to kickoff. We never invent a fake line.
QAfter I bet, when do I get paid if I win?+
The game settles automatically when it ends — winnings land in your balance right away (no “claim” button). Track it in My Bets under Open · Settled. In a hurry? Cash out before it ends.
QWhy can't I withdraw all my money? (bonus)+
Bonus funds can't be withdrawn or transferred (only money you deposited yourself). So withdrawable = total balance − bonus. Tap “Max” on the withdraw screen to auto-fill exactly what you can take out.
QThe 6-digit code didn't arrive.+
Check your spam folder. Still nothing? Tap “Resend code” after 30 seconds.
QI forgot my password.+
On the login page tap Forgot password? — you'll get a reset link by email to set a new one.
QA new tab logs me out.+
For security, login is per-tab. A new tab/window needs to log in again. (Refreshing the same tab keeps you signed in.)
QHow do I change the language?+
Top-right Settings (⚙️) → Language. Supports English, Korean, Japanese, Chinese, Spanish. You can also change time zone and odds format (American/Decimal) there.
QHow do I turn USDT into cash?+
Wallet → Withdraw your USDT to an exchange (Coinbase, Binance…) or MoonPay, then sell it — the cash lands in your bank.
QWhat is MoonPay?+
A card payment service to buy/sell crypto (USDT) with Visa · Mastercard · Apple Pay. It's the “Buy with card” option on the deposit screen. You can also sell USDT there to cash out.
QWhat is MetaMask?+
A personal crypto wallet app (browser extension / mobile app) that holds and sends coins like USDT. On Alpexa it's an alternative for buying USDT where MoonPay isn't available.
