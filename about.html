A message from our CEO
Office of the Chief Executive · Alpexa Suisse

When we started Alpexa, we had spent years on the other side of the screen — trading, and being let down by brokers who treated the spread as a place to hide. We decided to build the opposite.

Our promise is simple. You see the same price our engine sees. Your orders are filled at the market, not at a number invented to work against you. And your funds are held separately from the company’s — because your money is yours, full stop.

We also believe a modern trader shouldn’t need five apps. FX, stocks and crypto now live on one account, one platform, one clear view — so you can act on an idea the moment you have it.

Whether you are opening your first $100 account or managing serious size, you will be treated with the same honesty. Thank you for trusting us with your trading. We are only just getting started.

Christian Rossi Christian Rossi Chief Executive Officer · Alpexa FX
