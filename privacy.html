Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Alpexa ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use the Service. By using the Service, you consent to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

  • Identity & contact: name, date of birth, country, email address, phone number.
  • Verification (KYC): where required, identity documents and related data to meet legal obligations.
  • Account & transaction data: balances, deposits, withdrawals, trades, and wagers.
  • Technical data: device, log, and usage information needed to operate and secure the Service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and operate your account and provide the Service.
  • To verify your identity and age and to comply with anti-money-laundering, sanctions, and other legal obligations.
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and prohibited activity.
  • To communicate with you about your account and, with your consent, about products and promotions.

3. Legal Bases

We process personal data where it is necessary to perform our contract with you, to comply with legal obligations, for our legitimate interests in operating and securing the Service, or based on your consent (which you may withdraw at any time).

4. Sharing

We may share data with identity-verification and payment providers, with regulators and law-enforcement where legally required, and with service providers who process data on our behalf under appropriate safeguards. We do not sell your personal data.

5. Retention

We retain personal data for as long as your account is active and thereafter as required to meet legal, regulatory, accounting, and dispute-resolution obligations.

6. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data, object to or restrict certain processing, and request portability. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

7. Security

We use technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including server-side authority over balances and access controls. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure; you are responsible for safeguarding your credentials.

8. Marketing & Communications

We send promotional messages only where you have opted in, and you may opt out at any time. Account, security, and transactional messages are not optional while you hold an account.

9. Children

The Service is not directed to anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors and will delete such data if discovered.

10. Changes

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified through the Service.

11. Contact

Privacy questions or requests: privacy@alpexa-sports.com.
