Risk Disclosure

Last updated: 27 June 2026
Trading and wagering carry a high level of risk and may not be suitable for everyone. You can lose some or all of your funds. Only use money you can afford to lose.

This Risk Disclosure summarizes key risks of using the Alpexa Service. It is not exhaustive. You should not engage in any product you do not fully understand.

1. Risk of Loss

The value of crypto-assets, currencies, and other instruments can move sharply against you. Sports wagers can result in the total loss of the staked amount. Past performance is not indicative of future results.

2. Leverage

Leveraged products (e.g., FX and CFD-style instruments) magnify both gains and losses. A small adverse market move can result in losses that exceed your initial margin. Positions may be liquidated automatically without further notice.

3. Crypto-Asset Risk

  • Crypto-asset prices are highly volatile and can change at any time, including outside normal hours.
  • Blockchain transactions are generally irreversible; sending to a wrong address may result in permanent loss.
  • The Alpexa native token (ALPXS) is an in-platform asset whose value is determined by the platform's pricing model, not an external exchange.

4. Sports Wagering Risk

Odds and outcomes are uncertain. Wagering should be treated as entertainment, not as a source of income or investment.

5. Liquidity & Execution

At times you may be unable to open, close, or settle positions at your preferred price due to volatility, connectivity, or market conditions. Prices used for execution are the platform's server prices.

6. Technology Risk

The Service depends on internet connectivity, third-party infrastructure, and software. Outages, delays, or defects may affect your ability to transact. Balances are determined by Alpexa's servers, which are the authoritative record.

7. No Investment Advice

Alpexa does not provide investment, legal, tax, or financial advice. Any information provided is general and not tailored to your circumstances. Seek independent professional advice where appropriate.

8. Responsible Gaming

Set limits and play within your means. You may request deposit limits, time-outs, or self-exclusion. If gambling stops being fun, take a break or seek help from a qualified support organization.
